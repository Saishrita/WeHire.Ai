Privacy Policy – WeHire AI

Effective Date: September 21, 2025

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

WeHire AI Inc. (“WeHire AI,” “we,” “our,” “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our AI-driven recruitment platform (“Platform”).

By accessing or using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms & Conditions.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information from job seekers, employers, and recruiters:

a. Job Seekers

  • Name, contact details, location
  • Resume/CV, employment history, skills, education, certifications
  • Preferences (job type, salary expectations, work location)
  • AI interaction data (assessment responses, profile matching, interview scheduling)

b. Employers & Recruiters

  • Business name, contact details, job postings
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communications with candidates and usage analytics

c. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information, IP address, browser type
  • Cookies, usage logs, and analytics data
  • Interaction with AI features (recommendations, assessments)

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 in compliance with COPPA.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data for purposes such as:

  • Recruitment Services: Matching candidates to job opportunities using AI.
  • Platform Operations: Account setup, authentication, customer support.
  • AI Training & Improvement: Improving algorithm fairness, accuracy, and reducing bias.
  • Compliance: Ensuring adherence to EEOC, Title VII, ADA, ADEA, and state-specific employment laws.
  • Marketing & Communication: Sending updates, offers, and recommendations (with opt-out options).
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detecting unauthorized access or misuse.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (US Compliance)

We rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: When you create an account or submit your data.
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide recruitment services.
  • Legal Obligations: Complying with employment, privacy, and anti-discrimination laws.
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving AI fairness, security, and user experience.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Employers & Recruiters: Only for legitimate hiring purposes.
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics vendors.
  • Regulatory Authorities: When required by law, court orders, or government requests.
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

We do not sell personal data. Under CCPA/CPRA, you may opt out of any data sharing that could be classified as a “sale” or “sharing for targeted advertising.”

5. Candidate Rights

Depending on your state, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request details on data we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your data, subject to retention laws.
  • Right to Correct: Fix inaccurate information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Decline targeted advertising or automated profiling.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal access to services regardless of exercising privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@wehire.ai. We may verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

6. AI Fairness & Transparency

Because our platform uses AI for candidate screening and matching, we commit to:

  • Bias Monitoring: Regular audits to detect and minimize algorithmic bias.
  • Human Oversight: Employers make final hiring decisions; AI recommendations are advisory.
  • Transparency: Explaining factors that influence AI recommendations when reasonably possible.
  • Opt-Out: Users may opt out of AI-driven matching and request manual review (where applicable).

7. Data Retention

  • Candidate profiles: Retained for 2 years or until deleted by the user.
  • Employer data: Retained for as long as accounts are active.
  • Transaction records: Retained as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • AI training data: Anonymized before long-term retention.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized staff only.
  • PCI DSS Compliance: For payment data.
  • Incident Response: Notification of breaches in compliance with state breach notification laws (e.g., California, New York).

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Site functionality and user preferences
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Personalized job recommendations
  • Advertising (with opt-out options via cookie settings or browser controls)

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children, in compliance with COPPA. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us to request deletion.

11. International Users

Our Platform is intended for US users. If you are accessing from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different data protection standards than your country.
